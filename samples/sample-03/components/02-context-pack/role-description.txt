JOB DESCRIPTION: Senior Product Designer
Company: FlowSpace (example)
Location: Remote (UTC-5 to UTC+3)
Level: Senior (5+ years)

ABOUT THE ROLE
We're looking for a Senior Product Designer to own the end-to-end design of our core task management experience. You'll work closely with the product and engineering teams to translate user research into elegant, accessible, and high-performing designs.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Own the design of 2-3 major product areas simultaneously
- Conduct user research (interviews, usability tests, surveys) and translate findings into design decisions
- Create wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Collaborate with engineers to ensure designs are implemented with fidelity
- Maintain and evolve our design system
- Mentor junior designers on craft and process
- Present design rationale clearly to non-designers (PMs, executives, customers)

REQUIRED SKILLS & EXPERIENCE
- 5+ years of product design experience in a SaaS environment
- Strong portfolio demonstrating end-to-end design process (research → shipped product)
- Expert-level proficiency in Figma
- Experience working in agile/sprint-based teams
- Strong written and verbal communication
- Data-driven: comfortable interpreting analytics and A/B test results

NICE TO HAVE
- Experience designing for mobile (iOS/Android)
- Familiarity with HTML/CSS (enough to collaborate effectively with front-end engineers)
- Experience building or maintaining a design system at scale

SUCCESS IN FIRST 90 DAYS
- Complete a full design sprint on an existing product problem
- Ship at least one major feature with full design ownership
- Establish a relationship with at least 5 customers through research sessions
- Contribute at least one meaningful improvement to the design system
