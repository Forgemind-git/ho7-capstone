FLOWSPACE — COMPANY VALUES

These values are not aspirational posters — they are behaviours we hire for, promote for, and occasionally exit people for.

---

VALUE 1: CUSTOMER OBSESSION
We build for users, not for demos. Every design decision must trace back to a customer problem we've observed directly. We do user research before we write tickets, not after we've shipped.

Behaviours this looks like:
- Proactively seeks customer feedback; doesn't wait to be asked
- Can cite specific customer quotes or data to justify design choices
- Pushes back on features that don't solve a real observed problem

---

VALUE 2: CRAFT WITHOUT EGO
We take our work seriously but not ourselves. We ship great work, iterate quickly on feedback, and never defend our designs at the expense of the customer.

Behaviours this looks like:
- Receives critique openly and iterates without defensiveness
- Treats engineer and PM input as signal, not noise
- Prefers a good idea from anyone over their own mediocre idea

---

VALUE 3: DEFAULT TO ASYNC
We are a remote-first company. We write things down. We communicate with enough context that someone in a different timezone can act without a follow-up call.

Behaviours this looks like:
- Writes thorough design documentation and decision records
- Does not rely on verbal-only communication for important decisions
- Respects others' time by coming to meetings with clear pre-reads

---

VALUE 4: GROW THE PIE
We succeed as a team. Senior people are expected to make the people around them better — through mentorship, feedback, pair work, and shared knowledge.

Behaviours this looks like:
- Actively mentors more junior colleagues
- Shares learnings publicly (Slack, Notion, team retros) not just in DMs
- Celebrates team wins, not just personal wins
