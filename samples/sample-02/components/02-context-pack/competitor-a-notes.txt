ACME CORP — RELEASE NOTES & FEATURE OVERVIEW
Source: acmecorp.io/changelog + acmecorp.io/features
Last captured: 2024-10-30

=======================================================
RECENT RELEASES
=======================================================

October 2024 — v5.2
- Native offline mode for mobile apps (iOS and Android): work without internet, sync on reconnect
- AI-powered task summaries: one-click summary of any project with action items highlighted
- Gantt chart view: drag-and-drop timeline view now available on all paid plans

September 2024 — v5.1
- Custom automations builder: no-code automation with if/then rules, 15 built-in triggers
- Guest access: invite external collaborators with view-only or comment-only permissions (up to 5 guests free)
- Figma embed: embed live Figma frames directly in task descriptions

August 2024 — v5.0 (Major release)
- Redesigned dashboard with customisable widgets
- Time tracking built-in (previously required third-party integration)
- Workload view: see team capacity at a glance, flag overloaded members
